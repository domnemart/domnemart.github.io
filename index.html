domen martinčič is a director and cinematographer based in ljubljana. his work spans documentary, short and feature film, with a focus on intimate human stories and striking visual language.

he studied film directing at the academy of theatre, radio, film and television in ljubljana, and has collaborated with producers and directors across central europe.

his films have screened at various international festivals and have received recognition for their cinematographic approach and emotional depth.
