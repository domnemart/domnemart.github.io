Domen Martinčič is a filmmaker and director of photography. He studied film directing at AGRFT Ljubljana and later cinematography at ADU Zagreb, earning master's degrees in both. His work — as director and DoP — has screened at festivals including Karlovy Vary IFF, Sarajevo Film Festival, and Thessaloniki IDFF. He works in film, video, and theatre.
